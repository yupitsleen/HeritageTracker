## 5. Systematic Targeting Patterns

### Religious Sites (Targeting Palestinian Identity)

**Our Database (69 sites representing 140-160 buildings):**
- **13 Mosques** (Muslim) - majority destroyed
- **3 Churches** (Christian) - 2 heavily damaged, 1 surviving
- **Total Houses of Worship:** 16 sites, 11 destroyed (69%)

**Historical Pattern (1948):**
- **2,350 Islamic and Christian sites** desecrated in 1948 territories
- **200 of 313 large mosques** fell under Israeli control
- **100 mosques** completely demolished with villages
- **140 mosques** abandoned, many converted to synagogues, restaurants, nightclubs

**Key Insight:** Israel doesn't distinguish between Muslim and Christian Palestinians. Churches that survived are also Palestinian heritage under attack. St. Porphyrius (5th century, world's 3rd oldest church) was attacked twice.

### Museums & Knowledge Erasure (Intellectual Genocide)

**Gaza Genocide (2023-2025):**
- **12 Museums** in our database
  - **Al-Israa University Museum:** 3,000+ artifacts looted, building demolished with explosives
  - **Rafah Museum:** 30 years of collecting - destroyed
  - **Al-Qarara Museum:** 3,000 artifacts - destroyed

**Historical Pattern (1948-Present):**
- **70,000 books** looted from Palestinian homes (1948)
- **30,000 still marked "AP" (Abandoned Property)** in Israeli National Library
- **120,000 artifacts smuggled** from West Bank since 1967

**Impact Statement:**
> "They're not just destroying buildings—they're erasing Palestinian knowledge. 12 museums destroyed means 3 generations of scholarship, artifacts, and memory—gone. Then they display it in Israeli museums marked 'abandoned.'"

### Archaeological Sites (Erasing Pre-Islamic History)

**Pattern:**
- **10 archaeological sites** in our database (Byzantine, Roman, Bronze Age)
- Many pre-date Islam by thousands of years
- **Byzantine Church of Jabaliya** (5th century) - destroyed

**Impact Statement:**
