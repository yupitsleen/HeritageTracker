## Constraints & Challenges

### Known Limitations

1. **Documentation Gaps**
   - First/Second Intifada may have limited heritage documentation
   - 1956 Suez Crisis poorly documented for heritage
   - Pre-1948 Gaza sites may lack specific names

2. **Source Accessibility**
   - Some archives require institutional access
   - Palestinian government archives may be difficult to access
   - Israeli military archives are classified

3. **Verification Standards**
   - Must maintain 3-source minimum for credibility
   - Some historical sites may only have oral testimony
   - Coordinate precision decreases for older sites

4. **Scope Management**
   - Focus on Gaza Strip only (exclude West Bank)
   - Prioritize documented destruction over "at risk" sites
   - Balance comprehensive coverage vs. verification quality
